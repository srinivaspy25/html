About INDIAN University

INDIAN University is a research university located inytown, USA Founded in 1892, the university has a rich history of academic excellence and innovation. Our mission is to provide a world-class education to our students and to contribute to the advancement of knowledge through research and scholarship.

INDIAN University is home to over 30,000 students and offers a wide range of academic programs, from undergraduate majors to doctoral degrees. Our faculty are experts in their fields and are dedicated to teaching and mentoring our students. We are committed to providing a diverse and inclusive learning environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and intellectual curiosity.

At INDIAN University, we believe that education should be accessible to all. We offer a variety of financial aid options and scholarships to help make college affordable for our students. We are also committed to sustainability and have implemented numerous initiatives to reduce our carbon footprint and promote environmental stewardship.
